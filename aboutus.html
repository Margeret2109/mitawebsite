Our Background

Mita Cake House was founded in 1978 with a passion for baking and a vision to bring joy to every celebration through handcrafted desserts. Starting as a small neighborhood bakery, we quickly became a beloved destination for our unique, high-quality cakes and pastries. Over the years, our commitment to excellence and creativity has allowed us to grow while staying true to our roots of spreading sweetness and happiness.

Our Product

At Mita Cake House, we offer a delightful range of freshly baked goods, including signature cakes, custom creations, cupcakes, pastries, and cookies. From elegant wedding cakes to everyday treats, our products are crafted with premium ingredients and personalized to suit any occasion. Celebrate life’s sweetest moments with us!

Visit Us

Our cozy and inviting bakery is located at Ground Floor, 19, Jalan Setia Raja, Tabuan Laru, 93350 Kuching, Sarawak, offering a perfect spot to explore our wide range of cakes, pastries, and desserts.
